Habitism has two core concepts: `Task` and `Habit`.

## Task

Tasks occur randomly, such as attending a wedding.

You can use this app to record tasks.

## Habit

Habits are regular, such as eating and sleeping.

You can use this app to cultivate good habits, such as exercising.

You can use this app to quit bad habits, such as masturbation addiction.
